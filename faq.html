1. Can I book and hold a reservation and pay later?

No. Hold reservation is not possible throughout our website.

2. Can I make a group booking through your site?

Yes, for making a bulk or group booking.

3. How do I pay for my booking?

You can make payment through the following mode: We can accept iCashCard and all visa,Mastercard and most major debit card issued in India, as well as Net Banking payments from most major Indian banks for online bookings.

4. Does your site accept foreign(Non-Indian)Credit Card?

International cards are not acceptable.

5. When do I charge my credit card after I have made a booking on-line?

The ticket booking process will be complete after only the payment has been made by the guest. your payment will be processed immediatly.

6. Can I make a booking if I live outside of India

Yes definitely, using applicable credit card(Currently Indian cards-Mastercards/Visa). All our faces are in Indian Rupees. Credit cards payment will be converted at your credit card's current exchange.

7. Can I get a refund if I miss my flight?

If you miss your flight,it would be considerd a "No Show" and refund would depends on the airline policy. No Show charge will be applicable depending on the policy setup by airlines.

8.Can I reschedule my journey? if yes, what are the charges?

Most flights provide an option of rescheduling your journey. Rescheduling charges may apply based on the Airline policy. Additionally there will be an administrative charge of Rs 250. You may reschedule your tickets by calling our airlines directly.

9. Is there a special fare for senior citizens and students for tickets booked through your site?

Generally there is no such options but in cases where the airline or buses or railway providing such discounts the same will be passed on to the customer.

10. Do I have to confirm about my booking before travelling?

You could always call and check with the airlines or buses or railway and confirm the booking in case of any doubts.

11. Can I change my username?

The user name is a unique identity and cannot be changed. For a new user name, you will need to register on the website as a new member.
